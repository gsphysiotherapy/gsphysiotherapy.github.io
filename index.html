I'm Alexander Fomin (MPT, BaEXSS), I have over 5 years of powerlifting experience and treating athletes/patients.

At Grounded Strength Solutions, I provide physiotherapy and powerlifting coaching, combining clinical expertise with athletic experience to help lifters perform at their best, stay injury free, and build lasting strength.

With a Bachelor of Sports Science and a Master’s in Physiotherapy from the University of Sydney, I bring evidence based knowledge and hands on experience. As both a coach and competitor, I lead from the front so I can better understand and guide the athletes I work with.

Over the past 5 years, I’ve worked with powerlifters and treated physiotherapy patients across all levels.

  • Coached powerlifters from local meets to the international stage, including multiple national record holders across several weight classes.
  • Competed internationally, winning two medals at the 2022 Commonwealth Championships in bench press and deadlift
  • Helped athletes overcome chronic pain and go on to perform on some of the sport’s biggest stages

By combining my experience as a competitor, coach, and physiotherapist, I’m able to connect with lifters of all levels and help them move past setbacks toward lasting success.
